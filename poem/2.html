Hazaaron khwahishein aisi ke har khwahish pe dam nikle

ہزاروں خواہشیں ایسی کہ ہر خواہش پہ دم نکلے

By Mirza Ghalib

Couplet 1
ہزاروںخواہشیںایسیکہہرخواہشپہدمنکلے
بہتنکلےمیرےارمانلیکنپھربھیکمنکلے
Hazaaron khwahishein aisi ke har khwahish pe dam nikle
Bohat nikle mere armaan lekin phir bhi kam nikle
Thousands of desires, each so intense that it takes my breath away;
Many of my yearnings were fulfilled, yet they feel all too few.

Couplet 2
نکلناخلدسےآدمکاسنتےآئےہیںلیکن
بہتبےآبروہوکرترےکوچےسےہمنکلے
Nikalna khuld se aadam ka sunte aaye hain lekin
Bohat be-aabroo ho kar tere kooche se hum نکلے
We have always heard about Adam's expulsion from Paradise, but
With far greater disgrace did I depart from your street.
