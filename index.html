Poem of the Day

Dil-e-nadaan tujhe hua kya hai

دلِ ناداں تجھے ہوا کیا ہے

By Mirza Ghalib

Couplet 1
دلِناداںتجھےہواکیاہے
آخراسدردکیدواکیاہے
Dil-e-nadaan tujhe hua kya hai
Aakhir is dard ki dawa kya hai
O foolish heart, what has happened to you?
After all, what is the cure for this pain?

Couplet 2
ہمہیںمشتاقاوروہبیزار
یاالٰہییہماجراکیاہے
Hum hain mushtaq aur woh bezaar
Ya ilaahi yeh maajra kya hai
We are full of desire and they are indifferent;
O Lord, what is this affair?
